Nature and extent of personal selling

The function and task of personal selling

Tasks and duties of the salesperson differ from one business to another and there are different sales approaches, the following are universal to most selling businesses:

  • Finding prospective buyers

    • This is an essential prerequisite for succesful selling. Prospecting forms the basic and the first step in selling.

  • Converting prospects into customers

    • When a prospective buyer has been found, the task of the salesperson begins by showing the buyer how the product or service will satisfy their needs.

  • Follow-up, evaluation and after-sales service

    • This is assurance of customer satisfaction obtained by continual follow-up abd feedback
